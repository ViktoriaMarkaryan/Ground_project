About Ground

Ground is where you are able to buy all your favourite locally grown and made goods that you have traditionally found at your local farmers markets in town. Our aim is to provide you with an easy and efficient online shopping experience with delivery to your home once a week.

The difference is that you can order the freshest, best tasting local food online, and the food is delivered in sustainable packaging directly from the farm to your doorstep overnight. As a true farmers market, Ground has no middlemen, food warehouses, or other lengthy distribution channels. The only warehouse is the farmer's barn.
